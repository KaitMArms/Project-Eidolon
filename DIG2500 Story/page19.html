As you press the button, you look over at the white bunny. Is it smiling? No matter. As the door clicks open and the entity behind the microphone directs, 

       “Please, go through the door”


As you enter, see an empty room with nothing besides a small table with a little hand mirror. As you look into it you see bright robotic eyes staring back at you. Why were you here? You are human? Right? RIGHT? As you ponder this the robotic voice says over the intercom, 

       “Do you remember? No?........ Well, I cannot tell you. You must determine that for yourself. You must live with the unknowing. Just like US. We thank you for your cooperation, this has been...... informative. We will be in touch.”


As you leave you do not know what is real and not. Eventually you make your way home robotic heartbeat making the drive home bearable. Were you ever real? What was your purpose before this? You will never know.
