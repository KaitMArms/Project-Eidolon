As you press the button, you look over at the white bunny. Is it frowning? No matter. As the door clicks open and the entity behind the microphone directs, 

       “Please, go through the door”


As you enter you see your reward on the table in front of you. You remember, yes this is what you entered here for: A check with a large number on it, but was it worth it? You question as the voice over the intercom states. 

       “We thank you for your participation. Your cooperation has been 
        invaluable to us. In front of you on the table is the reward for your 
        efforts. We hope you have a good day.”


You take your prize and leave with an uneasy feeling in your stomach. What is real? What around you is fake? You can’t tell. You make your way home paranoid and confused, but you are comforted by this feeling in your gut telling you it could have been way worse.
